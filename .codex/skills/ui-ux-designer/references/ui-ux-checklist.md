# UI UX Checklist

## Product Fit

- Confirm target user and primary task are explicit.
- Confirm the design solves one measurable problem.
- Confirm non-goals and excluded behaviors are documented.

## Flow Quality

- Confirm entry points and end states are defined.
- Confirm empty, error, and retry paths are specified.
- Confirm irreversible actions have confirmation or undo.

## Interaction Quality

- Confirm component states are defined: default, focus, loading, error, success, disabled.
- Confirm form validation timing and error placement are explicit.
- Confirm click/tap targets are appropriate for desktop and mobile.

## Accessibility

- Confirm keyboard navigation works for all interactive elements.
- Confirm visible focus style is defined.
- Confirm labels, helper text, and error text are explicit.
- Confirm contrast expectations are documented.

## Responsive Behavior

- Confirm layout intent for small, medium, and large breakpoints.
- Confirm content wrapping or truncation behavior for long text.
- Confirm important actions remain visible on small screens.

## Handoff Readiness

- Confirm user flow, wireframe, and component specs align.
- Confirm terminology and labels are consistent across screens.
- Confirm acceptance criteria are testable by engineering and QA.
