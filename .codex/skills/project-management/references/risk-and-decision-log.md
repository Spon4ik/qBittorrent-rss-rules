# Risk and Decision Log

## Risk Entry Template

Record one risk per entry.

```md
### Risk: <short name>
- Trigger: <what condition introduces this risk>
- Impact: <what fails or slips>
- Mitigation: <what to do now>
- Owner: <person or role>
- Review date: <YYYY-MM-DD>
- Status: <open|monitoring|closed>
```

## Decision Entry Template

Record decisions with enough detail for future readers.

```md
### Decision: <short name>
- Date: <YYYY-MM-DD>
- Context: <what decision was needed>
- Options considered: <brief list>
- Chosen option: <selected path>
- Reasoning: <why this option won>
- Consequences: <tradeoffs and follow-up tasks>
```

## Usage Rules

- Keep one source of truth per decision; link to code or plan sections.
- Close risks only after mitigation is verified.
- Mark superseded decisions instead of deleting history.
